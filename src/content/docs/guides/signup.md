The Hills SGA is a golf association organized for senior golf playdays.  The group plays on Monday and Friday mornings beginning at 9AM during our season which runs from March to December.  Interesed in joining our group?

#### Requirements:
* Must be 55+ years of age
* Must have an established USGA handicap (GHIN)
* We can work with you to establish a handicap if you do not have one
* Dues of $70 added to your bill once a year

#### How to Join:
* Contact the pro shop and they can assist with adding you to the group within Golf Genius
* Download Golf Genius app and verify you are on our membership list
* Questions? - contact Joe Davies jos.davies77@gmail.com

#### Playdays
Once you are on the SGA membership list within Golf Genius, you will receive a email several days before each event.  Follow the instructions in the email to indicate that you would like to play in that playday.  The deadline for signups will be part of the email communication.   

Hope to see you soon on the course!
